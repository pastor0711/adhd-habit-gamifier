# 🎯 Goal Streak Tracker

A beautiful, ADHD-friendly web application to track your goals and build lasting habits. Monitor how long you've been working on each goal with automatic day, hour, minute, and second tracking, plus motivational features to keep you going!

**Created by [@pastor0711](https://github.com/pastor0711)**

## ADHD-Friendly Features

### 🎯 Granular Achievement System
- **Micro-achievements**: Get rewarded every 5, 15, 30 minutes in your first hour!
- **Hourly milestones**: Progress tracked at 1hr, 3hr, 6hr, 12hr on day one
- **25+ unique badges**: From 🌱 "Just Started" to 💯 "100 Days" and beyond
- **Day-specific badges**: Special achievements for days 1, 2, 3, 7, 14, 21, 30, 60, 100, 365
- **Animated badges**: Pulsing effects that catch your eye

### 🎨 Personalization & Organization
- **6 color themes**: Purple, Blue, Green, Orange, Red, and Teal for each goal
- **Color-coded cards**: Each goal has its own visual identity
- **Sort options**: View by Newest, Oldest, Longest Streak, or Shortest Streak
- **Overall statistics**: See total goals, active goals, total days, and longest streak

### 🔊 Multi-Sensory Feedback
- **Sound effects**: Cheerful tones when you add goals or hit milestones (toggle on/off)
- **Visual celebrations**: Animated emojis pop up for milestone achievements
- **Progress bars**: Animated shimmer effects show your journey
- **Motivational messages**: Rotating encouraging quotes for every goal

### 📝 Tracking & Accountability
- **Notes feature**: Journal your thoughts, feelings, or progress for each goal
- **Pause/Resume**: Life happens! Pause goals without losing your progress
- **Best streak tracking**: Your personal record is always visible
- **Real-time updates**: Numbers update every second so you see constant progress

### 💫 Visual Clarity
- **Big, bold numbers**: 2.5rem gradient text that's impossible to miss
- **Hover effects**: Every element responds to interaction
- **Clear hierarchy**: Important info stands out
- **Progress visualization**: See exactly how close you are to the next milestone
- **Reduced motion support**: Respects accessibility preferences

## How to Use

### Getting Started
1. **Open the App**: Simply open `index.html` in any modern web browser
2. **Pick a Color**: Select a color theme from the color picker
3. **Add a Goal**: Type your goal name and click "Add Goal" or press Enter
4. **Watch It Track**: The timer starts immediately and updates every second

### Managing Your Goals
- **Change Colors**: Click 🎨 Color to customize each goal's color theme
- **Pause/Resume**: Click ⏸️ Pause to temporarily stop tracking (perfect for breaks!)
- **Add Notes**: Click 📝 Notes to journal about your progress or feelings
- **Reset**: Click 🔄 Reset to start over (your best streak is saved!)
- **Delete**: Click 🗑️ Delete to remove a goal

### Organizing Your View
- **Sort Goals**: Use the dropdown to sort by newest, oldest, longest, or shortest streak
- **Dark Mode**: Click 🌙 to toggle dark mode (preference is saved!)
- **Toggle Sound**: Click 🔊 to turn sound effects on or off
- **View Stats**: See your overall progress at the top of the page

### Milestones & Achievements
- **First Hour**: Get badges every 5-15 minutes as you start
- **First Day**: Hourly achievements up to 24 hours
- **Long Term**: Special badges at 1, 2, 3, 7, 14, 21, 30, 60, 100, and 365 days!

## Technical Details

- **Real-time Updates**: Timers update every second automatically
